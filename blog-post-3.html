Why Edurown Exists

The story behind the platform and why students deserve better than expensive middlemen.

The academic support industry is broken. Here's why Edurown exists.

The Problem

On most platforms, a student pays $100 for an essay. The writer receives $25. The platform takes the rest. That's 75% gone.

Students pay premium prices but get unpredictable quality. They have no idea who is writing their papers. They deal with anonymous "writers" who may or may not deliver.

The Solution

Edurown was built on a simple idea: cut out the middlemen. Let students work directly with a qualified expert.

When students work with Edurown:

  • They pay less — no 75% markup.
  • They get better quality — work done by a Carnegie Mellon-admitted engineer.
  • They know who they're working with — no anonymous bidding pools.
  • They get direct communication — WhatsApp, not a ticket system.

What Edurown Offers

  • Academic Writing: Essays, research papers, dissertations, case studies, and term papers
  • Data & Statistical Analysis: Data visualization, statistical testing, and clear write-ups
  • Technical Projects: Web development, coding, and applied coursework
  • Lab & Technical Reports: Bioimage analysis, cybersecurity reports, and more
  • Editing & Proofreading: Line-by-line review for clarity, grammar, and citations

Why Students Deserve Better

Students are stressed, overwhelmed, and struggling. They pay too much for work that doesn't meet expectations. The industry takes advantage of their desperation.

Students deserve fair prices. They deserve quality work. They deserve to know who is helping them.

What Edurown Guarantees

  • Free sample: Send your topic, get 300 words written before you pay.
  • Milestone payments: 50% upfront, 50% when you're happy with the draft.
  • Unlimited revisions: Tweak until it matches your brief — no extra charges.
  • Direct WhatsApp: Chat directly. No tickets, no bots.
